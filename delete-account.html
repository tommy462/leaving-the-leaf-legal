Account & Data Deletion – Leaving The Leaf

If you would like your account and associated data deleted, please follow the steps below.

How to request deletion

Email: support@leavingtheleaf.app

Subject line: Account Deletion Request

What will be deleted

  • Account information
  • Daily check-ins and progress data
  • Journal entries
  • Any associated in-app personal data

Data retention

Requested account and associated user data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

Some limited information may be retained where required for legal, fraud-prevention, or security purposes.

Contact

If you have any questions, contact: support@leavingtheleaf.app
